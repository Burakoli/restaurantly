Nasi goreng

Nasi goreng is distinguished from other Asian fried rice recipes by its aromatic, earthy and smoky flavour.

Nasi goreng is traditionally served at home for breakfast and it is traditionally made out of leftover rice from the night before. The texture of leftover cooked rice is considered more suitable for nasi goreng than that of freshly cooked rice which may be too moist and soft to withstand frying in a wok.
